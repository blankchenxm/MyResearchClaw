这个方向已经从实验室可行性进入可穿戴原型与早期产品探索阶段。Ring 1 有公开 code / dataset；Ring 2 出现了 AlterEgo、Engram、SilentSpeak 这类明确面向 AI 交互和私密输入的产品化尝试；但在连续语音、长期佩戴舒适性、跨用户校准和法规上仍然远未成熟。
This direction has moved beyond lab-only feasibility into wearable prototypes and early product exploration. Ring 1 includes public code and datasets; Ring 2 already has product-facing attempts such as AlterEgo, Engram, and SilentSpeak aimed at AI interaction and private input; but continuous speech, long-term wear comfort, cross-user calibration, and regulation are still far from solved.
如果你对“骨传导 + EMG + 大模型”感兴趣，最值得关注的是感知链路并不只靠一个模型，而是 EMG sensing、个体化校准、语言模型纠错和骨传导回传共同组成闭环。
If you are interested in the “bone conduction + EMG + LLM” route, the key observation is that the system is not just one model: EMG sensing, personalization/calibration, language-model correction, and bone-conduction feedback form the closed loop together.
目前最大的缺口仍然是部署侧：公开结果大多还在有限词表、受控句子或短时佩戴下测试，而真正产品化需要稳定处理长时漂移、汗液/皮肤接触变化、无声口型歧义和多语言泛化。
The largest gap is still deployment: most published results are evaluated on limited vocabularies, controlled sentences, or short wear sessions, whereas productization requires stable handling of long-term drift, sweat/skin-contact variation, silent articulation ambiguity, and multilingual generalization.

Ring 2

Independent engineering solutions Independent engineering solutions

AlterEgo

product
Official product site
AlterEgo 是当前“骨传导回传 + 静默输入”路线最标志性的产品化尝试，直接把 silent speech 和 AI 交互叙事绑在一起。
AlterEgo is the most visible productization attempt of the bone-conduction-plus-silent-input route, directly tying silent speech to AI interaction.
它的系统意义不只是识别准确率，而是证明了 around-ear / jawline form factor 与 bone-conduction feedback 的完整交互闭环。
Its significance is not only recognition accuracy, but proving a full interaction loop that combines an around-ear/jawline form factor with bone-conduction feedback.

Engram

product
Silent-speech interface for AI
Engram 明确把产品定位成“对 AI 静默说话”的接口，说明 silent speech 已经开始直接对接 AI agent 场景。
Engram explicitly positions itself as a “silent speech interface for AI,” showing that the field is already targeting AI-agent interaction directly.
从工程角度看，这种产品强调的是实时性、佩戴形态和 prompt-style 命令集，而不只是学术识别指标。
From an engineering point of view, products like this emphasize latency, wearable form factor, and prompt-style command sets instead of only academic recognition metrics.

SilentSpeak

product
Mouth words, get text
SilentSpeak 的卖点是“默念/默动口型后直接得到文本或翻译结果”，这代表更直接的交互产品路线。
SilentSpeak’s pitch—mouth words silently and get text or translation—represents a more direct interaction-product route.
它说明 silent speech 不一定非要走脑机接口叙事，EMG + 私密输入本身就足够构成产品定位。
It shows that silent speech does not need to lean on a brain-interface narrative; EMG plus private input is already enough for a product story.

For the around-ear/headphone direction you care about, products like this are highly valuable adjacent engineering signals.
