Arnav Kapur et al.
IUI 2018 silent speech bone conduction wearable
摘要Summary
AlterEgo 仍然是“静默输入 + 私密反馈”这条路线最经典的系统论文之一，因为它把非侵入式神经肌肉信号采集和骨传导回传真正组合成了一个闭环设备。IUI 2018 这篇论文展示了一个可穿戴系统，能够在用户不张嘴、没有明显嘴唇动作的情况下，捕捉面部与颈部的细微神经肌肉信号，并在数字识别任务上报告 92% 的中位准确率。它对你这个题目最重要的意义在于，骨传导在这里不是输入，而是私密输出通道；输入侧则是对内在发音意图的解码。虽然当时还没有今天的大模型重打分或 agent 框架，但 AlterEgo 基本定义了后续很多 EMG silent speech 产品想要逼近的设备形态。
AlterEgo is still the clearest reference for a silent-speech system that combines non-invasive neuromuscular sensing with private bone-conduction feedback. The IUI 2018 paper describes a wearable that captures subtle facial and neck signals during internal articulation, without requiring the user to visibly mouth words, and reports a 92% median accuracy on a digit-recognition task. What makes it important for your topic is the closed-loop architecture: silent input on the sensing side, and bone-conduction audio on the output side. Even though the language modeling stack is much older than today's LLM pipelines, AlterEgo established the product shape that many later EMG+AI systems are converging toward.

A Cross-Modal Approach to Silent Speech with LLM-Enhanced Recognition
★ 0
Breakthrough 明确把 EMG 表征与 LLM 重打分结合起来，是“EMG + 大模型”路线的重要拐点。It explicitly combined silent-speech sensing with LLM-enhanced decoding, making it a key breakthrough for the EMG-plus-LLM direction.
Tyler Benster et al.
arXiv 2024 silent speech emg llm
摘要Summary
这篇论文是“EMG + 大模型”方向里与你需求最直接对齐的一篇。它先用 MONA 做 EMG 与语音表征之间的跨模态对齐，再引入 LISA 这个基于大语言模型的重打分层，来提升开放词表静默语音识别的结果质量。论文报告在 Gaddy 2020 基准上把 WER 从 28.8% 降到 12.2%，在 vocal EMG 记录上更把 23.3% 降到 3.7%。从工程角度看，这篇工作的核心启发不是“让 LLM 直接替代 EMG 解码器”，而是把大模型放在后端做语言约束、候选重排和错误修正。
This is the most directly relevant paper for the “EMG plus large model” direction. It introduces MONA for cross-modal alignment between EMG and audio representations, then adds LISA, an LLM-based scoring adjustment layer, to improve decoding quality on open-vocabulary silent speech. The paper reports a drop in WER from 28.8% to 12.2% on the Gaddy 2020 benchmark, and from 23.3% to 3.7% on vocal EMG recordings. For engineering, the key lesson is that the LLM should not replace the signal model; it works best as a language-aware rescoring and correction layer on top of a strong EMG decoder.

Chenyu Tang et al.
arXiv 2025 silent speech emg headphones
摘要Summary
这篇论文的重要性在于，它把静默语音 EMG 从传统贴片式面部电极，往更像消费设备的耳机形态推进了一步。系统把四个纺织电极直接集成进耳罩，用 ESP32-S3 做无线采集模块，再通过 1D SE-ResNet 动态适应皮肤-电极耦合变化带来的通道质量波动。在 10 个常见静默控制词上，它做到 96% 的准确率，这对可穿戴指令接口已经相当有代表性。对你关心的“骨传导 + EMG”路线来说，这篇工作虽然输入侧仍是 EMG，但在设备形态上已经明显朝着 around-ear、可结合私密音频回传的产品方向靠近。
This paper matters because it pushes silent-speech EMG toward a product-like headphone form factor instead of adhesive facial patches. The system integrates four textile electrodes into headphone earmuffs, uses an ESP32-S3 wireless readout module, and applies a 1D SE-ResNet to adapt to channel quality changes caused by skin-electrode coupling. On ten common voice-free control words, it reaches 96% accuracy, which is strong for a wearable command interface. If you are interested in bone-conduction-plus-EMG devices, this is one of the clearest bridges toward an around-ear consumer design, even though the paper itself focuses on EMG input rather than bone-conduction output.

Sentence-Level Silent Speech Recognition Using a Wearable EMG/EEG Sensor System with AI-Driven Sensor Fusion and Language Model

Consolidation 把句级识别、EMG/EEG 融合和语言模型纠错放到同一系统里，起到承上启下的收敛作用。It unifies sentence-level decoding, EMG/EEG fusion, and language-model correction in one system, making it a consolidation node.
Nicholas Satterlee et al.
Sensors 2025 silent speech emg eeg
摘要Summary
这篇工作值得纳入，是因为它没有停留在孤立词分类，而是把低通道可穿戴设备真正推进到了句级静默语音识别。系统同时使用单通道 EMG 和 EEG，用 moving-window few-shot Siamese 模型去切分连续生理信号，再在后处理阶段叠加统计语言模型来修复词汇和语法错误。论文在四条军事指令组成的句集上报告了 95.25% 的句级识别率。它用的还不是今天意义上的大语言模型，但系统结构上已经非常接近“先做信号解码，再用语言先验纠错”的 EMG+LLM 工程范式。
This paper is useful because it shows a sentence-level silent-speech pipeline on a low-channel wearable setup instead of stopping at isolated words. The system combines single-channel EMG and EEG, uses a moving-window few-shot Siamese model to segment continuous biosignals, and then applies a statistical language model as post-processing to repair lexical and syntactic mistakes. It reports 95.25% sentence-level recognition on a four-sentence military command set. The model is not an LLM in the modern sense, but it demonstrates the exact systems logic that later EMG+LLM stacks follow: first decode biosignals, then use language priors to clean up the sequence.

Masakazu Inoue et al.
Interspeech 2025 silent speech eeg emg
摘要Summary
这篇 Interspeech 论文解决的是落地时最真实的一个痛点：一旦电极布局变了，很多静默语音模型就会明显掉性能。作者训练了能处理 heterogeneous EEG/EMG 电极配置的网络，并在更大规模的汇总数据上做多任务训练，达到健康被试 95.3% 的词分类准确率，以及一位言语障碍患者 54.5% 的准确率。论文还报告了跨语言校准的提升。对工程实现来说，这篇工作的价值不在于“多刷几个点数”，而在于它正面回答了设备重新佩戴、跨用户、跨语言这些真实部署场景中的鲁棒性问题。
This Interspeech paper tackles one of the most practical deployment problems: silent-speech models usually break when the electrode montage changes. The authors train networks that can handle heterogeneous EEG/EMG placements and show strong multi-task transfer on a larger pooled dataset, reaching 95.3% word classification for healthy users and 54.5% for a speech-impaired patient. They also report gains in cross-language calibration. For engineering, that makes it an important reference on how to survive real-world variation in sensor placement rather than only optimizing a lab-perfect setup.

Fiona Meier et al.
arXiv 2025 silent speech neckband low power
摘要Summary
如果你更关心的是“能否做成低功耗、可长期佩戴的静默输入设备”，而不只是算法分数，这篇工作非常值得看。它采用 fully-dry 的纺织颈带，提供 14 路差分 EMG 通道，并基于 BioGAP-Ultra 平台把总功耗压到约 22 mW，用于采集与无线传输。论文报告 vocalized 命令 87% 的平均准确率、silent 命令 68% 的平均准确率，并且专门评估了设备重新佩戴后的跨 session 表现。dry 电极、超低功耗、重戴鲁棒性，这三点正是产品化时最关键的工程约束。
This work is one of the strongest references if you care about low-power, wearable, day-to-day silent speech input rather than only algorithm benchmarks. It uses a fully dry textile neckband with 14 differential EMG channels and the BioGAP-Ultra platform, running at about 22 mW total power for acquisition and wireless transmission. The paper reports 87% average accuracy for vocalized commands and 68% for silent ones, then explicitly evaluates session-to-session performance under device repositioning. That combination of dry electrodes, low power, and remount robustness is exactly the kind of engineering detail that matters for a real product.

SilentWear: an Ultra-Low Power Wearable System for EMG-based Silent Speech Recognition
★ 0
Frontier 把 wearable EMG、超低功耗 MCU 推理和多次重戴适配连起来，是当前最完整的边缘部署路线。It combines wearable EMG sensing, ultra-low-power MCU inference, and repeated re-wear adaptation, making it one of the most complete frontier edge-deployment routes.
Giusy Spacone et al.
arXiv 2026 silent speech mcu edge ai
摘要Summary
SilentWear 是这组论文里“最像真正产品原型”的一篇，因为它把可穿戴 EMG 采集和端侧识别完整闭环起来了。系统使用 14 路差分纺织颈带电极、一个只有 15k 参数的 SpeechNet CNN，并在商用 MCU 上做到 63.9 微焦每次推理、2.47 ms 时延。论文还给出 20.5 mW 总功耗下超过 27 小时的连续运行能力，并展示了设备重戴后通过少量增量微调恢复准确率的策略。换句话说，这篇论文讨论的已经不只是“能不能识别”，而是一个真正嵌入式 silent speech 设备该长成什么样。
SilentWear is the most deployable-looking recent paper in this set because it closes the loop from wearable EMG sensing to on-device recognition. The system uses a textile neck interface with 14 differential channels, a 15k-parameter SpeechNet CNN, and an MCU implementation that reaches 63.9 microjoules per inference with 2.47 ms latency. It also reports more than 27 hours of continuous operation at 20.5 mW total power and shows a practical incremental fine-tuning strategy to recover accuracy after repositioning. In other words, this paper is not just about classification; it is about what an actual embedded silent-speech appliance could look like.
